OUTPATIENT DEPARTMENT


An OPD (Outpatient Department) consultation refers to a medical service where you visit a hospital or clinic for a doctor's opinion, diagnosis, or treatment without being admitted for an overnight stay. It is the first point of contact between a patient and the healthcare system for non-emergency issues.

Key Features of OPD Consultation

  • No Hospitalization: You return home the same day.
  • Cost-Effective: Generally cheaper than inpatient care (IPD) as you avoid room rent and nursing charges.

Standard Consultation Process

  1. Registration: Book an appointment at the physical registration counter or at theonline portal.
  2. Consultation:The doctor reviews your medical history, symptoms, and performs a physical exam.
