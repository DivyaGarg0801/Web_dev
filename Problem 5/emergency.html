EMERGENCY DEPARTMENT


The Emergency Department provides immediate medical attention to patients suffering from serious injuries, accidents, or life-threatening conditions. It operates 24*7 and is equipped with advanced medical facilities and trained staff.

Key Features of Emergency Department

  • 24*7 Availability: Emergency services are available round the clock.
  • Ambulance Support: Well-equipped ambulance services for rapid transport.

Emergency Treatment Process

  1. Patient Arrival: Patient arrives via ambulance or walk-in.
  2. Triage: Condition is assessed and priority is assigned.
  3. Immediate Treatment: Doctors and nurses provide urgent care.

Types of Emergencies Handled

  1. Accidents
  2. Cardiac Emergencies
  3. Burns and Fractures
